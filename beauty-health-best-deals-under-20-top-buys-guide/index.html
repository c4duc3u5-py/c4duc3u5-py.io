What to look for when you buy beauty & health online (practical buying tips)

  • Check the condition and seals: “Brand new sealed” is preferable for creams, ointments, and salves to avoid contamination.
  • Inspect sizes and dosages: Note ml/g and compare per-ml price. A 60 g cream can be very different in cost-effectiveness to a 20 g tube.
  • Read ingredients and warnings: Especially for intimate, eczema, or pet products. Avoid essential oils if pregnant or allergic.
  • Verify seller details: Look for high-rated sellers, clear photos, and batch or expiry info. If expiry isn’t listed, message the seller.
  • Patch-test new topicals: Apply a small amount behind the ear or inner wrist for 24 hours before wider use.
  • Storage and care: Keep creams in a cool, dark place; sealing pads and wipes upright prevents drying out.
