What to look for when buying beauty & health on eBay UK

  • Ingredients and allergens: Always read the full ingredients list. For balms and salves, check for nuts, beeswax or essential oils if you have allergies.
  • Expiry and batch dates: Look for manufacture or expiry dates on listings. A sealed product with at least 12 months shelf life is ideal.
  • Seller reputation and returns: Prefer sellers with 98%+ positive feedback and clear returns/dispatch time.
  • Packaging and seals: New, sealed packaging is important for creams and oils; avoid listings without photos of seals.
  • Usage instructions: For medicated items (wart ointment, muscle creams) check for directions, contraindications and recommended course length.
  • Sizes and dimensions: Confirm ml/g and physical dimensions (9.5" stand, 130 ml toner, 60 sheets) to match your needs.
  • Skin testing: For exfoliants and active serums, patch-test on the forearm for 24 hours. Stop use if you see excessive redness or burning.

Quick buying tips and care

  • Store oils and balms in a cool, dark place to extend shelf life.
  • For toner pads: reseal the container immediately to prevent drying out.
  • Shaker sets: handwash and air-dry to keep finishes pristine.
  • For topical treatments: follow a clean-skin application routine and avoid combining multiple actives on the same area.
