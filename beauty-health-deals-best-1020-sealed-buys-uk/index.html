Buying tips before you buy beauty & health online UK

  • Check the seal and expiry/batch code before first use; unopened is always safer for creams and ointments.
  • Look for ingredient flags (steroid-free, fragrance-free) if you have eczema or sensitive skin.
  • For exfoliating pads and chemical toners, do a patch test and avoid mixing strong acids with retinoids.
  • Wart/medicinal products need clear instructions and may require GP advice if you have diabetes or poor circulation.
  • Shipping: always check the seller’s postage options and estimated delivery on the eBay listing before checkout.
