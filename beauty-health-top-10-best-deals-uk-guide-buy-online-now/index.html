What to look for when buying beauty & health products on eBay UK

  • Ingredient transparency: Check active ingredients, especially in exfoliants and topical treatments. Avoid unknown chemicals and prioritise recognised actives (e.g., AHA, BHA, niacinamide).
  • Authenticity and seller rating: For devices and branded creams, confirm the seller’s feedback and return policy. Higher-rated sellers reduce the chance of counterfeit goods.
  • Size and concentration: Note the product size (60g, 60ml, 130mL, 4oz) and whether multiple units are included (e.g., 2 x 60ml oils). Compare price per ml/gram to judge value.
  • Materials & safety: For mouth guards and stands, check materials (BPA-free plastics, aluminium alloy) and look for hygiene instructions. For skincare, check expiry dates and preservative systems.
  • Use case and instructions: For wart treatments, anti-snoring devices and exfoliants, ensure instructions are included. If a product requires fitting (mouth guard) or medical advice (wart removal), factor in safety.
  • Packaging and storage advice: Oils and salves last longer in dark or airtight packaging. Toner pads should have a sealed tub to prevent drying.
  • Returns & shipping: Confirm postage cost, delivery time and the returns window if a product isn’t suitable. For health-related items, some sellers restrict returns — read the listing.

How to get the best results and care for these picks

  • Exfoliating pads: Start slowly (2–3 times a week), use sun protection, and avoid mixing with retinoids on the same night.
  • Oils and salves: Apply to damp skin (oils) or clean dry patches (salves). Keep lids tight and store out of direct sunlight.
  • Mouth guard: Follow fitting instructions and replace every 6–12 months or if material warps. Clean with a soft toothbrush and mild soap.
  • Wart ointment: Use as directed and stop if irritation worsens. Seek medical help if wart is painful, bleeding or on a high-risk area.
  • Metal goods (stand, shaker): Dry immediately after washing to preserve finish. For alloys, avoid abrasive pads.
