What to look for when you buy beauty & health on eBay UK

  • Ingredients and active levels: for topical treatments check for known actives (salicylic acid, retinoids, peptides) and patch‑test if you have sensitive skin.
  • Size and coverage: toner pads, creams and oils list volume — 60 sheets or 130 mL makes it easy to compare value.
  • Materials and fit: mouth guards and stands have specific materials (thermoplastic, aluminium alloy) — these affect comfort and durability.
  • Seller details and returns: prefer sellers with clear returns and fast dispatch. Shipping on eBay is often calculated at checkout.
  • Care instructions: store creams and oils away from heat, and follow reuse guidance for mouthguards and implements.

Buying tips and care advice for these beauty & health buys

  • Patch test topicals: apply a small amount behind the ear for 48 hours if you have sensitive skin.
  • Volume vs value: compare mL/oz to judge cost‑per‑use — toner pads and oil list volumes so you can calculate longevity.
  • Hygiene for shared items: mouthguards and shakers should be cleaned after each use; salves and creams are best dispensed with a spatula to avoid contamination.
  • Check return and dispatch times on eBay: many sellers post fast, but check the listing if you need it before an event.
  • Watch interactions: avoid combining strong acids and retinoids; follow usage notes for wart removers and tightening creams.
