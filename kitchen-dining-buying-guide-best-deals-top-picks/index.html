Premium/Collector pick — Vintage Solid Bronze Tableware Cutlery Set (Thailand) (£139.99, Brand New in Box)


Vintage Solid Bronze Tableware Cutlery Set Thailand - Brand New in Box — Price: £139.99. Condition: Brand New — Shipping: see seller on eBay for postage options.

Why buy: Solid bronze cutlery is a statement. Bronze keeps a warm tone, ages with a patina, and feels substantial in the hand. For a special dining experience or as a showpiece set, this is the top-of-range choice.

What to look for when you buy kitchen & dining on eBay UK

  • Condition: New, Brand New in Box and Pre-owned matter. For tableware, “brand new” means patterns are intact; pre-owned needs close photos.
  • Materials: Stainless steel vs bronze vs ceramic determine care and longevity. Stainless steel is low-maintenance; bronze needs polishing; printed ceramics are decorative.
  • Size & capacity: For cups and mugs, confirm diameter and volume if you want a morning coffee mug vs a demitasse. For cutlery, check length and thickness to ensure comfortable handling.
  • Dishwasher & microwave safety: If you rely on convenience, confirm these explicitly in the listing. Markets like Cath Kidston often recommend handwash to protect prints.
  • Returns & postage: Seller postage options and return policy remove risk — check them before buying.
