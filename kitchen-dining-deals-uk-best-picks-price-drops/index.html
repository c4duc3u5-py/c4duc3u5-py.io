What to look for when you buy kitchen & dining items on eBay UK

  • Condition clarity: “Brand new”, “New (other)”, “Pre-owned” all mean different things. Ask for extra photos if any ambiguity exists.
  • Shipping & returns: Check postage cost and estimated delivery, and whether the seller offers returns. For heavier or fragile items (bronze cutlery), courier and insurance matter.
  • Materials & markings: Look for stainless steel grade (18/10 vs 18/0), bone china or fine china marking for cups, and metal type for cutlery (bronze, silverplate, stainless).
  • Dimensions: Confirm length of forks/knives and cup volume if size matters for your use or storage.
  • Care instructions: Avoid dishwashers for coloured or plated finishes. For metal tools, immediate cleaning prevents corrosion or clogging.
