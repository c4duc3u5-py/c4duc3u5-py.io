## Buying tips and care advice for these beauty & health buys
- Patch test topicals: apply a small amount behind the ear for 48 hours if you have sensitive skin.  
- Volume vs value: compare mL/oz to judge cost‑per‑use — toner pads and oil list volumes so you can calculate longevity.  
- Hygiene for shared items: mouthguards and shakers should be cleaned after each use; salves and creams are best dispensed with a spatula to avoid contamination.  
- Check return and dispatch times on eBay: many sellers post fast, but check the listing if you need it before an event.  
- Watch interactions: avoid combining strong acids and retinoids; follow usage notes for wart removers and tightening creams.
