## What to look for when you buy kitchen & dining online UK
- Condition and packaging: "BNIB" or "Brand new" keeps resale and gifting value high. For vintage or rare 1970's items, look for original box and any manufacturer marks.  
- Materials: For flatware, confirm stainless steel grade; for ceramics, check whether the glaze or decoration is hand-applied or printed.  
- Dimensions & capacity: Mugs usually 250–350ml; cups and saucers vary — check listing photos with rulers or seller notes.  
- Care instructions: Confirm dishwasher safety and avoid harsh detergents on printed or gilt finishes. For novelty mugs with plastic parts, handwash to keep parts intact.  
- Shipping and returns: Boxed items are safer in transit; check seller feedback and combined postage if buying multiple items.
