## What to look for when you buy kitchen & dining items on eBay UK

- Materials: Stainless steel, ceramic, bronze and plastic all need different care. Stainless steel is low maintenance; bronze and plated metals need hand washing and occasional polishing.
- Condition and packaging: “Brand new” and “BNIB” are best for gifts. For pre‑owned items check for dents, scratches and photos of actual item.
- Dimensions: Cup diameter, fork/spoon length, and pot capacity (the spray gun’s 600ml is already useful info) determine fit and functionality.
- Seller info: Check seller feedback, return policy and postage cost. Shipping can change the value on lower‑priced items like the toast rack (£7.36).
- Practicality: Novelty mugs are fun but may not be microwave/dishwasher-safe. For cutlery decide if you want dishwasher compatibility (confirm steel grade).

Care tips:
- Hand wash bronze and gold-trimmed ceramics; dry thoroughly.
- For metal cutlery, use a mild detergent and avoid soaking painted/coated handles.
- For tools like the HVLP gun, flush and dry after use and store seals in a small bag to avoid loss.
