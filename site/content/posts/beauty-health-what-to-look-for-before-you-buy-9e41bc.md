## What to look for when buying beauty & health items online (practical tips)
- Condition and returns: For health devices, choose items described as New or Very Good and confirm the seller allows returns. Calibration or expiry matters for medical devices.  
- Materials and cleaning: For silicone forms and masks, check wash instructions — heat deforms silicone and chlorine weakens fabrics.  
- Sizing and fit: For bras, use underbust measurements rather than dress sizes. If uncertain, message the seller with your measurements.  
- Read listing photos: Look for close-ups of seams, labels and packaging. Brand authenticity matters for Revell kits and branded cosmetics.  
- Battery and power check: For monitors and electronic stands, clarify whether batteries are included and if chargers are original.  
- Allergies and ingredients: Always scan skincare ingredient lists and perform a patch test for new products.  
- Shipping and delivery: Confirm estimated dispatch times and whether the seller ships within the UK (important for hygiene-sensitive items).
