## What to look for when you buy beauty & health items on eBay UK
- Materials and care: For textiles look for fibre blends (nylon, elastane) and explicit wash instructions. For silicone and stainless steel, check for medical-grade or 18/8 stainless markings. These details tell you about durability and hygiene.  
- Size and fit: Measure yourself (band + bust for bras) and check seller size charts. For accessories like stands, confirm load limits and folded dimensions (mm/cm).  
- Seller credibility: Prioritise sellers with strong feedback and clear return/dispatch policies. Look for items with buyer protections and tracked shipping.  
- Packaging and hygiene: For personal items (bras, silicone forms) ensure listings state “brand new” and unopened packaging. For beauty tools, check whether the kit includes what you expect (e.g., jigger, strainer) and if replacements are available.  
- Extra costs: Check postage and handling fees. The listed price doesn’t always include delivery; confirm total cost to avoid surprises at checkout.  
- Aftercare: Silicone forms should be hand washed and stored flat; metal cocktail kits are best-dried immediately to prevent watermarks; aluminium stands resist rust but avoid strong acids/bleaches.

## Practical care and sizing tips
- Bras and silicone forms: Measure sitting and standing for best symmetry. Buy the bra to fit your rib cage snugly; you can often adjust cup volume with thicker or thinner pads. Wash the bra gently and clean silicone inserts separately.  
- Metal cocktail kits: Rinse immediately after use, dry thoroughly and polish occasionally with a microfibre cloth. Avoid citrus-based cleaners on polished finishes.  
- Model kits: Work in a well-ventilated area. Use modelling cement for polystyrene and enamel or acrylic paints designed for plastic. Invest in a small magnifier and fine brushes for rigging and small fittings.  
- Portable stands: Wipe with a damp cloth and check hinges regularly. If using while applying heated tools, keep the stand out of direct heat exposure.
