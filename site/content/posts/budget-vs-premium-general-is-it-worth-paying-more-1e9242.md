## What to look for when you buy “general” items on eBay
- Condition and exact wording: “Zcela nový”, “nově zapečetěná”, and “nový (jiný)” mean different things. If it’s not factory sealed, ask for exact missing parts.  
- Seller feedback and returns: A low-price gem is worth verifying with a seller who accepts returns or has excellent ratings.  
- Dimensions and materials: For models, measure finished size; for tools, check nozzle size and cup capacity; for tableware, confirm stainless grade or porcelain marks.  
- Provenance for premium pricing: High prices need documentation (maker mark, limited edition number, invoice).  
- Shipping and taxes: High-value items can attract significant insurance or import fees — factor those into the total cost.

## Practical buying tips and care
- Electronic gear (Osmo): Update firmware, verify battery health, and test motors before finalizing a purchase.  
- Model kits (Revell): Check for all sprues and decals; store in a dry place to avoid yellowing.  
- Tools (spray gun): Run a leak and spray test before critical jobs; clean immediately after use.  
- Tableware and glassware (sada, sklenice): Handwash gilded or painted items and keep porcelain sets in padded boxes for storage.
