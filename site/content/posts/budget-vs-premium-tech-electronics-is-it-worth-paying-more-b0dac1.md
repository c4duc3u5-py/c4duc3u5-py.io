## Buying tips — what to look for when you buy tech & electronics
- Verify compatibility first: router modem types, VR part model numbers, or licensed collector editions. Mismatched hardware is the most common regret.
- Ask questions and request photos: for used items, request close-ups of connectors, serials, and box corners.
- Check shipping & returns: for electronics, free or reasonable return windows are important; for fragile ceramics, insured shipping is essential.
- Look for seller ratings and feedback on similar items. High-volume sellers often give clearer descriptions and returns.
- Care & maintenance: ventilů (ventilation) matters for routers — keep airflow clear; delicate joints (kloubu) need careful handling; ceramic collectibles require gentle cleaning and humidity control.
