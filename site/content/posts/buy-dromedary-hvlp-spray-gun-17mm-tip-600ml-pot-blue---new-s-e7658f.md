## What to check before buying — Kitchen & Dining specific tips
- Compressor compatibility: ensure your compressor can supply 4–8 CFM at 30–40 psi (approx. 2–3 bar). HVLP performance collapses if the compressor is underpowered.
- Paint compatibility: confirm the gun will handle the paints you plan to use (water-based acrylics often need thinning; oil-based varnishes may require different thinning agents).
- Nozzle set and spares: check whether the listing includes spare needles/nozzles or whether you can buy replacements easily.
- Pot connection and seal type: a leak-free screw coupling is essential when spraying near kitchen surfaces.
- Included controls: confirm there's a regulator or at least flow/fan knobs to adjust spray for cabinet doors vs. broad panels.
- Cleaning tools: check for a cleaning brush and nozzle spanner; cleaning after each session is essential to avoid blockages.
- Returns and warranty: read the seller’s returns policy and any warranty details. Brand New reduces risk, but not every seller offers returns on tools.
