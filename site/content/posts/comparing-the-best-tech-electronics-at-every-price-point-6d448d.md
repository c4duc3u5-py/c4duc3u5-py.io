## What to look for when you buy tech & electronics online UK
- Condition wording matters: "Brand new", "New (other)", and "Opened – never used" are different. Buyers should check seller photos and return policy.  
- Postage and returns: Confirm UK postage cost and estimated delivery. Some sellers offer free postage; others add a courier fee.  
- Compatibility and dimensions: For stands and mounts, verify maximum device size (phone length, laptop width). A claim that fits "all phones" is rarely true.  
- Materials and protection: For mounts and stands, check for silicone pads, aluminium build, or TPU/PVC windows to protect devices.  
- Firmware and updates: For routers, ensure the seller allows you to reset the device and update firmware. Ask for original box and paperwork if buying "opened – never used."  
- Seller rating and returns: Prefer sellers with high feedback and a clear returns window. This is key on eBay UK.
