### Care tips if you buy this gimbal
- Store folded in the pouch to protect motors and bearings.
- Avoid exposing the magnetic clamp to credit cards or magnetic storage.
- Keep motor vents clear and don’t force manual rotation when powered off.
- Update firmware via DJI Mimo after purchase to get the latest stability and tracking improvements.
- Charge via USB‑C with a 5V/2A charger; avoid using hot chargers that could shorten battery life.
