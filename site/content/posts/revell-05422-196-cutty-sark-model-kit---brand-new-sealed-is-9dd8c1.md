## Care, build and maintenance tips for this Revell 05422 kit

- Store the sealed box flat and out of direct sunlight to avoid warping of decals and instruction paper.
- When building, use plastic cement for strength on hull joins; cyanoacrylate (superglue) on small fittings only.
- Paint thin coats and allow full drying before handling rigging points.
- Keep parts organised: sort sprues and label sections before cutting to avoid losing small fittings.
- For display, mount the finished hull to a solid base and control humidity to preserve painted wood stands.
