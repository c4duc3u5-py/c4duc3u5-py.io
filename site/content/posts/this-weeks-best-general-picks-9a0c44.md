### What to look for when you buy general items on eBay UK
- Check seller feedback and return policy — items priced under £100 still benefit from a 30-day returns policy if the seller offers it.
- Materials and dimensions matter: ceramic sets need chip-free edges, model kits list scale and parts count, metal stands list load ratings.
- For branded pieces (Cath Kidston, Revell), inspect photos for authenticity markers — pattern alignment, maker’s mark, sealed packaging.
- Care tips: hand-wash delicate ceramics, degrease and test spray guns on scrap before jobs, run a polish cloth on gold-rimmed glass to avoid wearing the gilt.

## Buying tips — practical checks before you click
- Inspect photos for box seals and maker’s marks on branded items (Cath Kidston, Revell).
- For used glassware, zoom in on rims and bases for chips and gilt wear.
- Confirm exact dimensions and capacities — mug capacity, model finished length, stand load capacity, spray pot volume are key.
- Ask sellers about included accessories (brushes for model kits; additional fittings for spray guns).
- For tools and hobby kits, factor in consumables (paints, glue, compressor) to the total cost.
