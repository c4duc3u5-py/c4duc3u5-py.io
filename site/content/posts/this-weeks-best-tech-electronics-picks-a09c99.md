## What to look for when you buy tech & electronics on eBay UK

- Condition vs price: "Brand new" and "Opened – never used" have different return and warranty implications. Confirm seller returns and warranty coverage.  
- Seller location & shipping: check whether the seller is in the UK; local sellers usually mean faster delivery and simpler returns.  
- Compatibility: for routers, confirm VDSL/ADSL support; for stands and bags, check actual dimensions.  
- Photos & description: demand clear product photos and measurements. If any dimension is missing, ask the seller before buying.  
- Care & longevity: aluminium stands last for years with simple cleaning; TPU windows degrade with UV and abrasion—store out of direct sunlight.
