## Buying tips — what to look for when you buy beauty & health online in the UK

- Brand and ingredients: For creams, salves and pads, always check full ingredient lists on the eBay listing for allergens, fragrance and active concentrations.  
- Packaging and quantity: Note sizes — 60 sheets, 130mL, 60ml bottles, 4oz tubs — so you know how long a product will last.  
- Condition and returns: All products above are listed as Brand new. Confirm the seller’s returns and UK shipping terms before you buy.  
- Sizing and fit: For devices like mouth guards, check whether the product is one-size or includes moulding instructions; poor fit reduces effectiveness.  
- Care and storage: Oils and salves should be stored away from heat to avoid rancidity; metal tools and shakers are best hand-washed and dried immediately.  
- Safety warnings: Wart removers and strong peels need careful application — avoid broken skin and consult a GP for sensitive areas.

Include keywords like "brand", "cream", "pad", "sheets" when you compare products: pads and sheets (META FORET) are ready-to-use; creams (Harvey Ross) are topical and short-acting; salves (Organic Herbal) are occlusive and long-lasting.
