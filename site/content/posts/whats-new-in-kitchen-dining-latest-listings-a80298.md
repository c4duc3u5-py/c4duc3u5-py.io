## What to look for when you buy kitchen & dining items on eBay UK
- Condition and photos: Look for clear, close-up photos of edges, joins and markings; boxed or "BNIB" items typically command higher prices but verify seals.  
- Material specifics: For cutlery, prefer 18/10 or 18/0 stainless steel stated in the listing; for ceramics, check if items are bone china, porcelain or earthenware and whether they’re microwave/dishwasher safe.  
- Collectibles and gilt: Vintage gilt decorations (like the Dema glass) are fragile — expect surface wear. Ask the seller about any chips, repairs, or re-gilding.  
- Measurements and capacity: Confirm cup capacity (ml), mug diameter/height, and for flatware, overall length — these affect storage and usability.  
- Shipping & returns: Because these are fragile or boxed items, pay attention to packaging notes and return policies. If shipping is calculated at checkout, compare seller ratings first.

## Care tips and sizing guidance
- Stainless steel cutlery: Hand-dry immediately to avoid water spots and maintain polish; avoid bleach.  
- Gilt glassware: Never use a dishwasher. Handwash with mild detergent and soft sponge. Store upright and avoid stacking.  
- Ceramic mugs and boxed sets: If the seller confirms dishwasher safety you can use it, otherwise handwash. Keep original boxes for resale value.  
- Spray gun tools: Clean immediately after use to prevent clogging; follow manufacturer instructions for thinners and nozzle maintenance.
