What to check before buying this bike phone mount frame bag

  • Confirm phone dimensions: measure your phone (with case) and compare to the bag’s internal fit. If you have a large phone (6.5"+), double-check the listing photos or ask the seller for internal measurements.
  • Inspect the mounting system: make sure your frame geometry will accept three Velcro straps; very thick top tubes or unusual frame shapes can affect fit. Ask the seller about strap length if in doubt.
  • Verify weather protection: check whether the zipper has a rain flap and whether the TPU window is sealed; for heavy rain you may still need an extra waterproof sleeve.
  • Check the seller’s shipping and returns: confirm postage cost, dispatch time and return window on the eBay listing to avoid surprises.
  • Look at real photos: request close-ups of the TPU window and stitching if the listing only shows stock images. Scratches or poor seam work may be reasons to return a unit.
